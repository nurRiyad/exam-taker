# ADR 0007: Start With BCS And Government Job MCQ Exams

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The platform could serve many exam categories, including government jobs, admission tests, and school/coaching exams. A broad launch would weaken positioning and make question structure, marketing, and teacher workflows harder to focus.

## Decision

Start with BCS and government-job preparation exams. Admission-test preparation can be considered later after the first workflow is working.

## Consequences

Benefits:

- Clear first customer segment.
- Strong match with recurring MCQ exams.
- Easier to write landing copy, teacher pitch, and question tags.
- More coherent weak-zone analysis across exams.

Tradeoffs:

- Some nearby teachers outside the segment may be deferred.
- The product should avoid hardcoding only BCS-specific assumptions so admission tests remain possible later.
