# ADR 0029: Require Teacher Content Responsibility Confirmation Before Upload

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Teachers may upload questions copied from guidebooks or other copyrighted sources. The platform needs a trust and risk boundary before accepting uploaded/imported content.

## Decision

Teachers must confirm once per account that uploaded/imported questions are open/source-safe or owned/authorized by them, and that they are responsible for uploaded content.

## Consequences

Benefits:

- Sets clear responsibility.
- Creates a basic legal/trust boundary.
- Avoids annoying teachers with repeated confirmations on every import.

Risks:

- Teachers may accept terms without understanding.
- The platform may still receive complaints.

Future:

- Add takedown/report flow if copyright complaints appear.
