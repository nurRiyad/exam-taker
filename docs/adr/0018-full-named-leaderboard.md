# ADR 0018: Show Full Named Leaderboard For MVP

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Ranking is a core motivation in exam preparation. The founder wants students to see the full leaderboard with names.

## Decision

MVP shows a full exam leaderboard with student names.

## Consequences

Benefits:

- Strong motivation and competition.
- Mirrors coaching exam culture.
- Makes rank more meaningful than a private number.

Risks:

- Some students may feel exposed or discouraged.
- Privacy expectations may vary by teacher or student group.

Guardrails:

- Show name and rank, not phone/email.
- Consider hidden-name or initials-only options later if teachers request it.
