# ADR 0041: Show Unanswered Count And Confirm Incomplete Submit

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Students may accidentally submit with unanswered questions. On mobile, it is easy to miss a question while navigating.

## Decision

Before final submit, show the unanswered question count. If unanswered questions remain, require confirmation before submitting.

## Consequences

Benefits:

- Reduces accidental incomplete submissions.
- Gives students a final review moment.

Guardrails:

- Do not block auto-submit when time ends.
- Keep the confirmation fast and mobile-friendly.
