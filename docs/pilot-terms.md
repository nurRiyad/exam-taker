# First Paid Pilot Terms

## Offer

- Price: 500 BDT.
- Included: 5 live exams.
- Student cap: up to 100 enrolled students.
- Extra student baseline: 100 BDT per additional 50 students.
- Final price can be adjusted by agreement with the teacher before the pilot starts.

## Included Support

- Platform owner helps create the teacher page.
- Platform owner helps import or enter the first exam questions.
- Founder is available during the first live exams for urgent support.
- Teacher receives a basic branded page with logo, banner, color, and teacher/user picture using the common template.

## Teacher Responsibility

- Teacher is responsible for the correctness of exam questions and answers.
- Teacher confirms uploaded/imported questions are owned by the teacher, authorized for use, or source-safe.
- Teacher handles student payment collection outside the platform.
- Teacher manually approves each student payment/access request.

## Platform Responsibility

- Platform provides the exam page, scheduled live attempts, ranking, result display, weak-zone reporting, and Bangla PDF print support.
- Platform will try to keep live exams reliable within the early budget guardrail.
- If Cloudflare free limits are hit during a pilot, the platform may upgrade to a paid limit while keeping total early monthly spend under 2,000 BDT unless agreed otherwise.

## Refund And Downtime

- If a platform-side failure prevents a scheduled live exam from running, that exam should not count against the 5-exam pack.
- If the teacher cancels or does not provide questions on time, the exam can be rescheduled but still belongs to the same pilot pack.
- No automatic cash refund policy is promised in MVP; failed platform exams are handled by replacement exam credit first.

## Data And Export

- Student results stay inside the platform in MVP.
- Teacher-facing full result export is not included in MVP.
- Question set CSV export is available as the MVP backup/export path for question content.

## Scope Limits

- No video classes.
- No automated SMS/OTP.
- No online payment collection.
- No proctoring.
- No teacher announcements/messages.
- No offline paper result entry.
